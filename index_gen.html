About

Ira is an artist based in Oban on the West coast of Scotland, he works in pen and ink, oils, pastels, charcoals and recently started to explore digital painting. Ira's work is inspired by the wildlife of Scotland and the world. He currently shares an open studio space with four other artists at www.artspaceoban.co.uk
